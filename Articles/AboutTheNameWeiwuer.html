About the name Weiwuer

2022-06-16 14:18:23

What is Weiwuer? The name "Weiwuer" is "Uyghur" written in Pinyin. Pinyin is anglicized Mandarin. It is not political, I just like the Uyghurs.

I originally wanted the Uyghur domain. Unsurprisingly, it was taken. As I still wanted something Uyghur-related, I opted for the Pinyin version. So, here I have it. Thanks to Pinyin, there are a lot of untapped domains that have cool meaning to those-in-the-know.
